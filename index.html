About Me.

A bit about my history with coding

Filipe Cancellier da Costa

Web Developer in training



I was born and raised in Brazil, I consider myself a good and a simple person, in my free time you will probably find me playing some game on my laptop, exploring the city or watching YouTube :D

My career can be summarized in engineering jobs predominantly. I have always worked in construction and mechanical field in Brazil. Although most of my professionals skills are turned to these fields, I felt that something wasn't right something was missing, I wasn't totally happy being a Civil Engineer, so I decided to challenge myself and try to learn something new.

I have always enjoyed computer, games and technology so I thought "Why not try coding? ", and so I did. I started enrolling in some free online programming courses and ended up starting with Python. I was enjoying learning Python but one day, my cousin who is a front-end engineer, showed me a little bit about web development, and that was so incredible that I decided to try learn some Javascript, just to see what it would be like. After I a few days learning Javascript, I really fell in love with all this, the freedom and limitless creativity that it gives to you, and the fact that you can see every line of code that you wrote working on the screen. And since then I've been learning web development trying to improve each day more.

Why FAC?

Founders and Coders



FAC is a program that creates incredible opportunities for people who are interested in starting a career in web development, they also provide an incredible community where the aim is for everyone to help and learn more together, being totally inclusive for everyone.

I have no doubt tha this opportunity is amazing. Since when I started studying web development I expected to get a job in the area because this is an activity that I really enjoy doing. And having the chance to achieve that in this community where I got the opportunity to meet great people and learn from them and also learn how to work together in the coding world, I think this is great! I've been enjoying each new step each new feature that I learned every day and I really want to take this to a new level, and that's why I chose founders and coders.
