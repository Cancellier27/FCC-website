About Me.

A new passionate for coding...

Filipe Cancellier da Costa

Web Developer in training



I was born and raised in Brazil, I consider myself a good and a simple person, in my free time you will probably find me playing some game on my laptop, exploring the city or studying coding.

My career is summarized in engineering jobs. I graduated in Civil Engineering in 2017, when I started working as a Civil Engineer in a city close to my hometown. Although most of my professionals skills are turned to this field, I felt that something wasn't right something was missing, I wasn't totally happy being a Civil Engineer, so I decided to challenge myself and try to learn something new.

I have always enjoyed computer, games and technology so I thought "Why not try coding? ", and so I did. I started enrolling in some free online programming courses and ended up starting with Python. I was enjoying learning Python but one day, my cousin, who is a front-end engineer, showed me a little bit about web development. So I decided to try some Javascript, just to see what it would be like. After I wrote my first “Hello World” in Javascript, I really fell in love with all this, the freedom and limitless creativity that it gives to you, and the fact that you can see every line of code that you wrote working on the screen.
